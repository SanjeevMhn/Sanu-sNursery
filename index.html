Excellent Planting Services

Here at Sanu's Nursery we provide all services regarding plants, starting from the seed to each and every stage in between. We take a great pride in every plant and aim to provide excellent care and dedication to every last one. We provide wide range of plants ranging from flowers, vegetables, shrubs, fruits and also upon request. We provide plants that are fully germinated stored in plastic cups ready to be planted in fields, gradens or even flowering pots. We also answer any query you may have regarding plants.

Plants and Shrubs

We provide seedlings for plants and small shrubs such as avocado, tomato, lemon, etc.

Fruits and Vegetables

We provide seedlings for vegetables such as cauliflower, brocolli, onion, pumpkin, cucumber, eggplant, etc.

Flowers

We provide ready to plant to flower seedlings of most in demand flowers according to the season such as Marigold, Rose, Sunflower, Tulips, etc.

Gardening Tools

We also provide various gardening tools such as water jugs, pipes, cocoa peat, mud flowering pot, plastic containers, plastic sheets, organic fertilizers, etc.

What we do

Here at Sanu's Nursery we combine traditional and modern techniques and technologies to our full advantage to create the best environment for our plants to mature and be of high quality. We work on improving each and every aspect through research and experience. We try and adopt new techniques but closely regard traditional knowledge as well. We practice safety and cleanliness at work.
